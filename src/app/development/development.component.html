Having fallen into computer science at a very young age, passionate about art and new technologies, the Web appeared to me as an ideal environment for the association of these two sensibilities. From mastering languages ​​useful for creating sites to mastering graphics software, I quickly felt cramped on this two-dimensional plan.
The discovery of 3D made me the effect of entering a new universe. A universe very similar to ours and it too is governed by physics and mathematics. The capacity of networks and the computing power of terminals have hitherto been a hindrance to the integration of the third dimension into the Web. But these barriers are now on the verge of falling, leaving the field open to a new way of expressing oneself, of experimenting, of classifying data.
This site is the result of a long training in programming languages ​​and graphical tools, guided by the desire to create 3-dimensional sites based on solid and innovative technologies. This platform is a "laboratory" having for objective, on the one hand, to present my work, and, on the other hand, to share my know-how.
